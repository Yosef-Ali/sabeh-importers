The Developer agrees to design, develop, and deploy a comprehensive digital solution for the Client as detailed in the proposal dated January 20, 2026. The scope includes:

- Responsive e-commerce website with admin dashboard.
- Features: User registration, product catalog, shopping cart, order tracking, payment integration (TeleBirr, CBE Birr), and customer support chat.

### 1.2. Mobile Applications
- **Android Application:** Native app published to Google Play Store.
- **iOS Application:** Native app published to Apple App Store.
- Features: Push notifications, synchronized data with web platform, mobile-optimized UI.

### 1.3. Admin Dashboard
- Centralized management for products, inventory, orders, customers, and sales reports.

### 1.4. Services
- UI/UX Design (Web & Mobile).
- Backend & API Development.
- Deployment to App Stores.
- Staff training and 3-month post-launch support.
